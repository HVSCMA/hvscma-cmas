Ranch Living Optimization

Consider enhancing the layout with wider doorways, accessible bathroom features, and improved flow between living spaces. This positions the property for the growing market of buyers seeking barrier-free living.

Potential Value Add: $15,000 - $30,000

Fireplace Area Enhancement

The existing fireplace provides a natural focal point for living area improvements. Consider creating a defined hearth area with built-in shelving, stone or tile surrounds, and improved lighting. This enhancement maximizes one of the home's key architectural features while creating a premium entertaining space.

Potential Value Add: $12,000 - $25,000

Garage and Storage Optimization

The attached garage provides opportunities for enhanced storage solutions and potential workshop space. Consider adding organized storage systems, workbench areas, or even climate-controlled storage for seasonal items. These improvements appeal to buyers seeking functional utility spaces.
