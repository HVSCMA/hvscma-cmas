This exceptional 5-bedroom Cape-style home represents one of the largest properties of its type in Pleasant Valley, featuring a rare master bedroom on the first floor and an impressive 3,579 square feet of living space. Built in 1962 and extensively renovated in 2005, the home combines classic Cape Cod charm with modern amenities throughout.

The property showcases gleaming hardwood floors, a brick fireplace in the family room, and a thoughtful layout with another bedroom or den on the first floor. Upstairs, three huge bedrooms with new carpet provide excellent space for family living. The finished lower level adds valuable storage and potential recreational space.

The outdoor amenities are equally impressive, featuring a 28' round pool and flat backyard that provides abundant space for outdoor enjoyment. The 1.2-acre lot offers exceptional privacy while maintaining convenient access to Pleasant Valley amenities and major transportation routes.
