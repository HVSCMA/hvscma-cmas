Living in Staatsburg, NY: A Local’s Guide to the Hamlet by the Hudson

Staatsburg is a small Hudson Valley hamlet with big advantages: riverfront parks, historic grounds, wooded trails, and quick connections to Hyde Park and Rhinebeck.

Lifestyle & Vibe

Expect a slower pace amid tree-lined roads and river breezes. Weekends often revolve around walks on the Mills Mansion grounds, scenic drives, and coffee in nearby villages.

Parks & Outdoors

Mills-Norrie State Park provides miles of trails, picnic spots, and Hudson River overlooks. Seasonal foliage is a highlight, and kayaking opportunities are nearby.

Dining & Essentials

Groceries and cafés line Route 9, with more choices in Hyde Park and Rhinebeck—everything from casual bites to destination dining.

Commuting & Access

Route 9 puts you minutes from Hyde Park and Rhinebeck; Rhinecliff Amtrak connects to NYC and Albany. Local roads make weekend exploring easy.
