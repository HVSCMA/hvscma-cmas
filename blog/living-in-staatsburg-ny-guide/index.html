Living in Staatsburg, NY: A Local’s Guide

Staatsburg is a quiet Hudson Valley hamlet set between Hyde Park and Rhinebeck along the Hudson River. Here’s what residents love.

Everyday Lifestyle

  • Walkable historic grounds at the Staatsburgh State Historic Site
  • Riverfront time at Mills-Norrie State Park
  • Nearby cafés and weekend markets

Commuting & Connections

Route 9 connects you to Hyde Park and Rhinebeck, while Rhinecliff Amtrak provides service to NYC and Albany.

Why it fits

If you’re after scenic calm with quick connections to dining, trails, and services, Staatsburg delivers.
