Weekend Itinerary: 24 Hours Around Staatsburg and the Hudson River

Use this simple itinerary to sample Staatsburg’s highlights without rushing.

Morning

Coffee and a calm walk on the Mills Mansion grounds with river views.

Midday

Trail time at Mills-Norrie or a scenic drive; lunch in Hyde Park or Rhinebeck.

Afternoon

Explore shops or galleries nearby; consider a short kayak outing.

Evening

Dinner with a view; sunset overlooks along the river or open fields.
