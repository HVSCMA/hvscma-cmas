Best Parks, Trails, and Riverfront Spots Near Staatsburg, NY

Outdoor time is effortless in Staatsburg. Start with Mills-Norrie State Park and branch out to hidden overlooks and quiet river bends.

Mills-Norrie State Park

Miles of wooded trails, campsites, and access to the Hudson make this the area’s anchor for hikers and picnickers.

Staatsburgh State Historic Site

Open lawns, river views, and seasonal events on the grounds of the Gilded Age estate, perfect for evening walks.

Dinsmore Golf Course

A scenic course on the state park grounds; even non-golfers enjoy the views and open skies.

River Access & Kayaking

Launch points and marinas nearby provide opportunities to kayak and explore calmer river coves.
