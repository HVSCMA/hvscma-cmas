Property Description

Embrace true luxury in this magnificent Colonial situated in the prestigious Legends at Beekman Country Club, just 70 miles from NYC. This elegant, professionally decorated home features over 7,500 square feet of living space with an expansive two-story foyer that opens to a sunken family room with soaring ceilings, gas fireplace, and wall of windows.

The well-appointed gourmet kitchen showcases maple cabinets, stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, center island, and butler's pantry. The home features elegant formal dining and living rooms, plus a sun-filled home office perfect for remote work. The spacious master suite includes a 2-sided fireplace, sitting room, spa-like master bath, and impressive 30-foot walk-in closet.

The breathtaking backyard features exquisite landscaping with specimen trees, a peaceful waterfall, expansive patio with built-in fireplace, gazebo, and outdoor kitchen. The property offers plenty of room for an in-ground pool and represents the perfect escape from city life to the beautiful Hudson Valley.
