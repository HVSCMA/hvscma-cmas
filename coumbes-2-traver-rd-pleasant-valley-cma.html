Only one comparable 5-bedroom property sold recently at $649,900. The additional bedroom commands a premium of $15,000-$20,000 in today's market, especially with the master bedroom conveniently located on the first floor.

Large Lot Advantage

The 1.2-acre lot provides exceptional privacy and outdoor space that's increasingly rare in the area. Properties with similar acreage command $25,000-$40,000 premiums over standard residential lots.

Pool & Entertainment Value

The above-ground pool and flat backyard create an immediate entertainment space that saves buyers $15,000-$25,000 in installation costs while providing instant summer enjoyment.

School District Appeal

Pleasant Valley's Arlington School District rating enhances property desirability and long-term value retention, particularly attractive to families seeking quality education within commuting distance to NYC.

Cape Cod Timeless Appeal

The Cape Cod architectural style with hardwood floors and period details appeals to buyers seeking character homes. This style has proven market resilience and broad buyer appeal across multiple demographics.

Privacy & Location Balance

The property offers rural privacy while maintaining 10-minute access to Pleasant Valley and 15 minutes to Poughkeepsie, providing the ideal balance of tranquility and convenience that defines Hudson Valley living.
